About Palawan

Palawan is located in the Western Visayas Region in the Philippines. Being a top destination for tourist and locals there are airline flights from Manila (capital of the Philippines) to Palawan every day, travel time is estimated to be about 30 minutes. There are many choices of hotels and resorts in Palawan which offers friendly hospitality, a cuisine sure to please your palate and world class services that will definitely give you an experience of a lifetime.
